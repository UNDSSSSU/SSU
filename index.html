The Special Services Unit (SSU) provides security coverage for HOS/HOG, Monarchs, Foreign Ministers, Senior UN Officials, and Celebrities.

The Unit communicates with the Host-Country to remain aware of any threats or demonstrations that could affect the security and safety of the United Nations.

SSU approves requests for temporary Security Grounds Passes for security components escorting VIPs, Dignitaries visiting the UN and handles the collection and delivery of petitions addressed to the Secretary-General.
