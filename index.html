The Special Services Unit (SSU) provides security coverage for HOS/HOG, Monarchs, Foreign Ministers, Senior UN Officials, and Celebrities.

Conducts close protection for visiting dignitaries within the UN Headquarters complex.​

Provides security coverage of special events held on the UNHQ premises and Presentation of Credentials.​

SSU collaborates with the Host-Country security entities during the visit of the VVIP dignitaries at the UNHQ including coordination of the VVIP escorted motorcade, host goverment law enforcement agencies national dignitary protection team at the UNHQ.

The unit also approves request for national Security Grounds Passes for accompanying VVIP Dignitaries visiting the UNHQ including approval of rental vehicles application as well as temporary driver passes.
